THE Second AWAKENING:
A Digital Revival of the American Soul

Reformers of the current age must look to the digital tools at their disposal to spread the gospel of progress. The press, once bound by iron and ink, now travels at the speed of light. We stand at the precipice of a new era where equality and justice are not merely dreams, but attainable goals for every citizen of the Republic.

“Reform is not inherited. It is rebuilt by every generation.”

The movement for temperance, equality, and abolition finds new breath within these coded columns. Let no person say the press has gone silent. We advocate for a labor force of sober, industrious character and a nation of liberated minds.
